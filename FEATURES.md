# UpcharSaathi - Feature Documentation

This document provides a detailed overview of the core features available in the **UpcharSaathi** application.

## 1. ü§ñ Medi LLM Chatbot
An intelligent, AI-powered health assistant designed to provide immediate medical guidance and answers.
-   **Natural Language Understanding**: Users can describe symptoms or ask health questions in plain English (or other languages supported by the LLM).
-   **Symptom Analysis**: Analyzes reported symptoms to suggest potential conditions (Disclaimer: Not a replacement for professional medical advice).
-   **Health Advice**: Offers general wellness tips, first aid suggestions, and preventive care information based on medical knowledge bases.
-   **Contextual Awareness**: Maintains conversation history for follow-up questions during the active session.

## 2. üìç Find Care
A comprehensive locator service to help users find medical facilities and professionals nearby.
-   **Find Doctors**: Search for specialists (e.g., Cardiologists, Dentists) based on location and ratings.
-   **Locate Hospitals**: Find the nearest hospitals and clinics with emergency services.
-   **Blood Bank Locator**: Real-time search for nearby blood banks with contact information.
-   **Geolocation Integration**: Uses device GPS to automatically show the closest results ("Near Me").
-   **Interactive Map**: Visualizes search results on a map for easier navigation.

## 3. üìà Track Health
A personal health monitoring dashboard to keep track of vital signs and fitness data.
-   **Vitals Logging**: Manually log heart rate, blood pressure, weight, and blood sugar levels.
-   **Wearable Sync**: Integration with **Fitbit** (and generic Health Connect) to automatically import steps, sleep data, and active minutes.
-   **Data Visualization**: Interactive charts and graphs to visualize health trends over daily, weekly, and monthly periods.

## 4. üß† Mental Health
Tools dedicated to emotional well-being and mental health support.
-   **Mood Tracker**: Log daily moods and emotions to identify patterns over time.
-   **Self-Assessments**: Standardized questionnaires (e.g., PHQ-9, GAD-7) to screen for anxiety and depression levels.
-   **Wellness Resources**: Access to guided meditations, breathing exercises, and mental health articles.

## 5. üöë First Aid Guide
A quick-reference library for emergency situations.
-   **Emergency Protocols**: Step-by-step guides for common emergencies like CPR, burns, choking, and fractures.
-   **Visual Aids**: Diagrams and clear instructions to assist users in high-stress situations.
-   **Offline Access**: Designed to be lightweight and accessible even with poor internet connectivity.

## 6. üë§ User Profile & Medical Records
A secure, centralized hub for personal health information.
-   **Medical History**: Store and organize past prescriptions, lab reports, and vaccination records.
-   **OCR Upload**: Upload images of paper medical reports; AI extracts textual data automatically for easy searching and storage.
-   **Personal Details**: Manage profile information, emergency contacts, and app settings.

## 7. üì∞ Health News
Stay informed with the latest developments in the medical world.
-   **Curated Feed**: Aggregates health news from trusted medical journals and news sources via RSS.
-   **Topic Filtering**: Filter news by categories like "Public Health", "Nutrition", "Medical Research", etc.
